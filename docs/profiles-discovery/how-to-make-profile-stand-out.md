# How can I make my profile stand out?

Some tips will help make your profile stand out:

- Use a clear profile photo, write a short but engaging bio, and link to your top platforms. Mention your content style, collaboration interests, and availability.
- If you are a performer make sure to fill up as many details as possible from your performer profile.
- Connect your Bluesky account to show case your work in your profile
